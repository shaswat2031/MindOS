# MindOS — Your Personal Decision & Mindset Engine
*"Think better. Decide faster. Grow daily."*

## The Core Problem
Most people know what they should do, but they struggle to execute. The gap isn't knowledge; it's **clarity, decision-making, and mindset.** MindOS is designed to be an affordable, focused tool to bridge this gap.

---

## Core Features

### 1. Decision Coach (AI-powered)
- Structured questioning to clarify life/career/money decisions.
- Moves beyond simple pros/cons to a *values-aligned* decision framework.
- Example: "Should I leave my job?" → App explores long-term goals and fears (failure vs. regret).

### 2. Daily Mindset Check-in (5 minutes)
- 3 daily questions to track patterns.
- Identifies where the user is stuck and detects limiting beliefs.
- A low-cost combination of journaling and therapy.

### 3. Focus Score + Habit Tracker
- Daily "focus score" based on check-in data.
- Visual progress, streaks, and weekly reviews for habit tracking.

### 4. Wealth Clarity Module
- Simple financial snapshot (income, savings, investment status).
- Projects current trajectories (e.g., "Where you'll be in 30 years").
- Focuses on clarity rather than complex investing.

### 5. Weekly Growth Report
- Personalized 1-page Sunday report.
- Summarizes decisions, mindset patterns, focus scores, and identifies one key action for the next week.

## Target Audience
- 20–35 year olds in India/South Asia feeling "stuck."
- First-generation professionals without mentors.
- Side-hustlers struggling with focus.
- Students making career-defining decisions.

## Why It Will Work
1. **Universal Pain Point:** Decision fatigue and mindset blocks are common.
2. **Untapped Market:** No strong competitor at this price point in emerging markets.
3. **Scalability:** AI-driven systems allow for mass personalization.
4. **Retention:** Daily habit-forming check-ins ensure high stickiness.
