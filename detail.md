## 💰 1. THE MVP ENGINE (PHASE 1: 0 → PMF)
We are cutting 80% of the features to achieve absolute clarity.

### 🔥 The Core Product: The Decision Engine
The entire MVP experience revolves around a single high-impact flow:
1.  **Input:** User enters a life/career/money struggle.
2.  **Structuring:** AI breaks down the decision into core variables.
3.  **Output:** 
    *   **Regret Analysis:** "What will you regret in 10 years?"
    *   **Risk Breakdown:** Quantifying the downside.
    *   **Recommendation:** A structured, values-aligned path forward.

### ❌ REMOVED (FOR NOW)
*   **Growth Circles:** Accountability is a Phase 3 feature.
*   **Wealth Module:** Financial clarity is a distraction for the MVP.
*   **Full AI Mentor:** Replaced by the focused Decision Coach.
*   **Gamification:** Streaks and XP are secondary to solving the user's immediate pain.
